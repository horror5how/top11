## What I Did Today

### 1. Added plain-HTML "Browse all rankings" nav to home page
**Problem:** The AI cluster (ai-coding-assistants, ai-agent-builders) has NO indexed peer page
to link to it. The home page links to all 54 lists, but those links are wrapped in `Reveal` 
(`opacity:0` until JS fires). While Google technically follows opacity:0 links, for a new 
domain with infrequent crawl schedule, a definitive HTML-visible crawl path matters.

**Fix:** Added a `<nav aria-label="Browse all rankings">` section directly before the footer
in `app/page.tsx` — pure server-rendered HTML `<Link>` elements, no Reveal wrapper, no JS
dependency. This gives Google an unambiguous 1-hop crawl path from the indexed `/` to all 54
list pages, including the AI cluster.

**Why this vs. relatedLinks.ts:** The `peerSlugs` approach already covers the CFO cluster 
from /fractional-cfo (server-rendered, no JS). The AI cluster is isolated — its only indexed
discovery path is through `/`. The footer nav fixes that.

### 2. Rewrote /dental-crm title + meta for CTR

Old title: `"11 Best Dental Practice Management Software (2026)"`

**Expected impact:** Same as /fractional-cfo fix. Even 1% CTR on 77 impressions/month ≈ 1 click/month now vs. 0. If position improves to top 5, the 77 impressions could grow significantly.
