## Red flags by brand (where any)
- **Kruze Consulting** (The 11 Best Fractional CFOs for Tech Startups #2): low. No verifiable data breach or lawsuit; a couple of isolated, years-old negative client reviews on Yelp sit against an otherwise positive 4.3/5 rating, so risk is low and not a current trend.
- **Pilot** (The 11 Best Fractional CFOs for Tech Startups #4): low. No verifiable breach or lawsuit; some genuine billing complaints on Trustpilot about charging small businesses corporate rates and upselling, but reviews overall skew positive (9/10 on TrustRadius).
- **Zeni** (The 11 Best Fractional CFOs for Tech Startups #5): low. No confirmed breach or lawsuit against Zeni; one TrustRadius reviewer alleges ACH fraud draining their account in October 2024, but this is a single unverified user allegation, not a proven Zeni security breach.
- **NOW CFO** (The 11 Best Fractional CFOs for Tech Startups #8): low. Only a few isolated, years-old client complaints about a non-refunded fee and a poor placement; no breach, lawsuit, or recent service-quality pattern found.
- **Paro** (The 11 Best Fractional CFOs for Tech Startups #9): moderate. Multiple independent platforms show a recurring client pattern of billing disputes and unresponsive support when an assigned expert fails, though no data breach or verified lawsuit against Paro was found. Paro is a marketplace, so experience varies by the individual expert matched.
- **The CFO Centre** (The 11 Best Fractional CFOs for Tech Startups #10): low. Predominantly positive public reviews (Trustpilot around 4 stars) with one isolated customer complaint about unclear communication; no breaches, lawsuits, or billing-fraud records found. As a franchise network, service quality is tied to the local partner.
